# SelfMax Guidance Copy Inventory (Consolidated)

Purpose:
- Capture high-value language that frames intent, reflection, and coaching.
- Exclude navigation labels, generic buttons, and structural UI chrome.

In scope:
- Instructional text blocks
- Reflective prompts/questions
- Goal-coaching copy
- Task-suggestion framing copy

Out of scope:
- Nav/menu labels, section headers with no instructional value
- Generic controls (e.g., `Add`, `View`, `Type your message...`)

## 1) Goal Guidance Intro (Goals Workspace)

- `Don't know where to start? Ask me (or tell me) anything! I'll guide you towards one of your goals, or help you make a new one.`

## 2) Lifestorming Framing Copy

- `Some people don't know what they want in life. Some of them don't even know that they don't know!`
- `LIFESTORMING is a big first step towards knowing what you want so you can go get it.`

## 3) Brainstorming Guidance Copy

- `First, you're going to think of as many things as possible that you might want in your life.`
- `Don't worry if they feel "stupid!" That's the point of brainstorming. Say everything that comes to mind.`
- `Spend a few minutes writing down anything you can think of to improve this part of your life. Anything! There are no wrong answers!`
- `Think of something, type it in, and click ADD. Stop when you feel like you have enough.`

## 4) Feel-It-Out Guidance Copy

- `Now that you have a list of DESIRES, take your time to imagine each one. What would it be like to go for it?`
- `How would it feel? What would you see? Who would you meet?`
- `Spend a few minutes on your DESIRES, one at a time, and take careful notes on how they make you FEEL.`
- `Take a few minutes to think about adding this DESIRE to your life.`
- `Pay attention to HOW YOU FEEL and write it down as you ask yourself each question:`

Reflective question set:
- `WHY do you want this?`
- `WHAT would you get out of it?`
- `HOW would you do it?`
- `WHAT would happen if you did it?`
- `WHERE would you go to do it?`
- `WHAT would you see, hear, and feel?`
- `HOW does it feel to imagine doing it?`
- `WHAT feelings come up for you?`
- `WHAT would it be like?`
- `Do you BELIEVE you can do it? If not, do you WANT to?`

## 5) Goal-Start Framing Copy

- `Now you know how you feel! When you're ready, pick a DESIRE to turn into a GOAL.`
- `Or, you can delete it if you don't want to make it happen. It's all about what you want!`
- `Define a specific, actionable goal you'd like to achieve. The Self-Maximize framework will help you reflect on it.`

## 6) Self-Maximize Diagnostic Prompts

DESIRE:
- `WHAT do you want?`
- `What do you NEED to get what you want?`

ENVIRONMENT:
- `WHERE can you get what you want?`

MENTALITY:
- `HOW are you thinking about this goal?`
- `What beliefs or assumptions are shaping your approach?`

ACTIONS:
- `What are you DOING for this goal?`
- `If others are involved, how do you APPEAR to them when you take action?`

SITUATION:
- `How is this goal going right now?`
- `What is your current situation, even if you haven't taken action yet?`

FEEDBACK:
- `What did you LEARN from your actions in your ENVIRONMENT?`
- `What SITUATION did they create?`

## 7) In-Goal Coach Example Copy (Dynamic, Useful for Style)

- `Hello, I’m here to help you explore your goal of starting a family. What specifically do you want your family life to look like, and why is this important to you right now?`

Notes:
- This is dynamic thread content, but valuable as a reference for tone and question style.
- Prefer templating this pattern as: `explore your goal of {goal_title} ... what specifically ... and why now?`

## 8) Task Suggestion Framing Copy

- `How will you accomplish: {goal_title}?`
- `Select the tasks you want to add:`
- `Tasks are generated based on your personality, demographics and previous answers. They are just suggestions and inspiration, and can be ignored or edited.`

## 9) Empty-State Guidance Copy

- `No items in this bucket yet.`
- `No active goals found.`
